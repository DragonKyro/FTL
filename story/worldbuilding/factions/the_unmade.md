# The Unmade

> *They tell you to listen. They don't tell you what listening costs.
> Or what it discovered. Or why the Vyrnari closed the door.* — Unmade
> proselytizing pamphlet

## Identity & origin

The Unmade are Choral Order heretics who broke from the Order roughly
sixty years ago. Their founding doctrine is simple and terrible:

> The Vyrnari did not vanish. They **chose** to retreat from the
> universe and sealed the Helix Gates behind them. They did this
> because *something on the other side of those Gates* would have
> consumed everything. They tried to leave the cosmos a sealed mausoleum.
> We are *undoing their sacrifice* by trying to re-open the Gates.

The Unmade believe that the only righteous act is to *complete the
Collapse*. Close every Gate. Seal every system. Let the galaxy go silent
and survive in fragments, the way the Vyrnari intended.

Their founder was **Hellion Cas-Mehlen**, a Choral Order Speaker who
heard, during a sixteen-day Echo trance, what he called "the *true*
last words of the Vyrnari." He recorded those words in a sealed text
and walked into a Gate that closed behind him three hours later. Most
of the Order considered his text a fabrication or a tragedy. His
followers considered it scripture.

The current Unmade leader is an unknown figure styled **the Hollow
Speaker**, who has never been positively identified by Consilium
intelligence. There are persistent rumors the Hollow Speaker is a
Whisperborn, which the Unmade themselves treat as the unforgivable
blasphemy.

## Goals

- Accelerate the Collapse.
- Destroy First-Tone Shrines wherever they are identified.
- Hunt Whisperborn — interpreted by the Unmade as Echo-rifts inhabited
  by *whatever is on the other side of the Gates*.
- Convert Echo-sensitive Choral members, especially Choir.
- *(Apocalyptic faction:)* prepare communities for the Long Silence
  they believe is coming.

## Methods

Terror, surgical and political. The Unmade *do* run a fleet, but it's
small and unconventional — repurposed civilian hulls covered in ritual
scarring, crewed by zealots, often willing to ram. Their preferred
attacks: bombing Choral shrines, assassinating Choir scholars, sabotaging
Verge Gate-restoration projects, kidnapping (and "freeing") Whisperborn.

The Unmade also *evangelize*. Their pamphlets are everywhere. Their
arguments are sophisticated; their conclusions are unbearable. They are
the Choral Order's mirror, and they know exactly which Choral doctrines
to invert.

## Internal tensions

- **Violence vs Persuasion.** Some Unmade cells are pure-conversion
  preachers. Others are bombers. The two cells generally despise each
  other.
- **The Hollow Speaker's identity.** Several senior Unmade believe the
  Speaker is a fraud or a Bureau plant. Several others believe the
  Speaker is the next Vyrnari incarnation. No one alive has met them
  to ask.
- **The Whisperborn paradox.** Doctrine: Whisperborn are vessels for
  the Outerlight and must be eliminated. Persistent rumor: the Hollow
  Speaker *is* a Whisperborn. The contradiction is, so far, ignored.

## Stance toward other factions

- **Consilium:** designated terrorist.
- **Iron Concordat:** designated terrorist; *publicly* hunted; *privately,
  Concordat Marshals have occasionally found Unmade Gate-sealings
  politically useful* and looked the other way.
- **Mercatile Verge:** mortal enemy. The Unmade attack Verge ships
  routinely.
- **Choral Order:** mortal enemy and original parent. The schism is
  personal as well as theological.
- **Verdantis:** complicated. Some Verdantis sympathize with the Unmade
  doctrine of "preserving what is by stopping change." The Verdantis
  publicly disavow this.
- **Splice:** despised.
- **Mhirsa Compact:** despised in turn; the Mhirsa have killed many
  Unmade attempting to bomb Mhirsa shrines.
- **Andrachen-in-Exile:** secretly tolerated. The Andrachen view the
  Unmade as useful destabilizers.
- **Drift Communes:** the Unmade have made *significant* inroads into
  some Drift Communes — apocalyptic isolationism plays well in habitat-
  arks.
- **Lattice:** mutual extinction-threat. Both believe the other is the
  abomination.
- **Hollow:** uncomfortably similar (uploaded minds, embodied; the
  Unmade reject the comparison furiously).
- **Wardens:** holy. The Unmade believe the Wardens are Vyrnari servants
  carrying out the original Sealing mandate. Some Unmade try to
  cooperate with Wardens; most die in the attempt.
- **Outerlight:** the Unmade are split. Most believe the Outerlight is
  the *threat* the Vyrnari sealed away. A minority believe it is the
  *Vyrnari themselves*, returning to finish what they started.

## Where you find them in-game

- Bombing or already-bombed Choral shrines.
- Boarding actions against Verge cargo haulers.
- Pamphlet-distribution events at neutral beacons (low-stakes encounter).
- Whisperborn hunts (very high-stakes encounters).
- Unmade fleets defending sealed Gate sites.

## Story hooks

- Encounter an Unmade preacher whose arguments you can't easily dismiss.
- Be hired by the Choral Order to hunt an Unmade cell.
- *Be* an Unmade — see the *Unmade Heresy* storyline, the dark route,
  including the question of whether to fight or let through the
  Outerlight at the final fight.
- Encounter the Hollow Speaker (only on the Whisperborn or Unmade
  storyline paths) and discover who they actually are.
