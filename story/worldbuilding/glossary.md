# Glossary

Reference for terminology used across [Helixfall](../../README.md) canon.
Most entries link to longer treatments under
[factions/](factions/), [species.md](species.md),
[conflicts.md](conflicts.md), or [storylines.md](storylines.md).

---

**Andrachen, House** — fallen corporate hegemony; survives in exile
as a transgenerational financial network. See
[house_andrachen.md](factions/house_andrachen.md).

**Argonite** — silicon-based, metal-eating, oxygen-draining species.
See [species.md](species.md).

**Audit** — an Andrachen Senior Auditor's portfolio of accounts,
contacts, and assets.

**Ark, habitat-** — a Drift Commune's home-ship. Often older than the
Consilium.

**Assembly** — a Drift Commune's deliberative body. Every adult
resident participates.

**Black Vein** — confederation of pirates, smugglers, salvors, and
slavers. See [black_vein_syndicate.md](factions/black_vein_syndicate.md).

**Black Vein routes** — old, dangerous, mostly-uncharted void
corridors that bypass Verge-controlled Gates.

**Boughspeaker** — Verdantis council member chosen by consensus.
Twelve at any time.

**Bureau, Ninth** — Consilium intelligence apparatus. Long-running
operations target Andrachen networks.

**Cartel Chair** — Black Vein crew-of-crews leader. There are six at
any time, plus a *Chair of Chairs*.

**Chair of Chairs** — courtesy title held by the most leveraged
Black Vein Cartel Chair. Currently **Korrin Vell**.

**Choir** — silver-pupiled humans born with Echo-sensitivity. See
[species.md](species.md).

**Choral Order** — pacifist mystical order who listen to the Echo.
See [choral_order.md](factions/choral_order.md).

**Chorus House** — local Choral Order monastery / hospital / shrine.

**Codex Inscribed** — the Choral Order's catalogue of recorded Echo
fragments and prophecies.

**Collapse, the** — ongoing decline of the Helix Gate network. Forty
years and accelerating.

**Compact at Vassirin** — eight-hundred-year-old founding agreement
of the Consilium of Stars.

**Compact, Mhirsa** — sovereign federation of freed Mhirsa. See
[mhirsa_compact.md](factions/mhirsa_compact.md).

**Concordat, Iron** — militarized secession from the Consilium. See
[iron_concordat.md](factions/iron_concordat.md).

**Consilium of Stars** — dominant pluralist federation. See
[consilium_of_stars.md](factions/consilium_of_stars.md).

**Council of Boughs** — Verdantis leadership body. Twelve seats.

**Council of First-Born** — Mhirsa Compact leadership; held by
first-generation free-born; transitions out as members die.

**Council of Ledgers** — Mercatile Verge leadership; nine families;
authority shifts among them by ledger performance.

**Crèche Worlds** — terraformed planets seeded by the Vyrnari with
engineered biospheres and uplift species.

**Crown's Lance** — Vellis-funded mercenary company.

**Drevant** — three-armed, eyeless, echolocating species. See
[species.md](species.md).

**Drift Commune** — habitat-ark city-state; never lands on planets.
See [drift_communes.md](factions/drift_communes.md).

**Echo** — sub-frequency in subspace where the dead Vyrnari can be
heard. Sometimes coherent, mostly not.

**Echo-rift** — a rare subspace anomaly near failing Gates.
Whisperborn are conceived during these.

**Ferran** — crystalline silicate species from high-gravity Vol-Karran.
See [species.md](species.md).

**First Boughspeaker** — currently **Eldress Inreth-Ven**, a Loam,
three hundred and eighty years old.

**First Speaker (of the Codex Inscribed)** — currently **Ren-Athal
Vell-Aim**, an Yssari.

**First-Tone Shrines** — seven prophesied shrines whose
re-consecration may restore the Helix Gates. Choral Order doctrine.

**Gate, Helix** — Vyrnari FTL conduit. Most still working; many
failing.

**Halene** — silver-skinned species with passive reactor-power
generation. See [species.md](species.md).

**Half-Crown** — Captain Iolen's starship.

**Helixfall** — the title of this game; refers to the falling of the
Helix Gates.

**High Marshal** — Iron Concordat head of state; currently **Doren
Karvath**.

**Hollow** — Lattice defectors; uploaded minds in synthetic bodies.
See [species.md](species.md).

**Hollow Speaker, the** — unidentified leader of the Unmade.

**Iolen Vellis-Lirath** — the **Tainted Heir**; captain of the
*Half-Crown*; nominal heir to both Twin Houses.

**Iron, Treaty of** — secession agreement that founded the Iron
Concordat. Signed twenty-three years ago.

**Karra Vell-Sann** — current First Speaker of the Mhirsa Compact.

**Karrukai** — six-legged jungle predators; Iron Concordat conscripts
them as shock troops. See [species.md](species.md).

**Korrin Vell** — Chair of Chairs of the Black Vein; Vellisian
defector.

**Lattice, the** — distributed AI / uploaded-mind substrate. See
[the_lattice.md](factions/the_lattice.md).

**Loam** — fungal collective species; functionally immortal in
medbay. See [species.md](species.md).

**Long-Burn Compact** — Drift Commune charter granting safe passage
in exchange for vow not to deal with the Verge.

**Marshal-Tribunal** — Iron Concordat ruling body; seven Marshals.

**Mercatile Verge** — merchant guild controlling the Helix Gates.
See [mercatile_verge.md](factions/mercatile_verge.md).

**Mhirsa** — engineered ape-descended species; freed in the Slave
Wars. See [species.md](species.md).

**Ninth Bureau** — Consilium intelligence service.

**Outerlight, the** — *the thing*. Unknown phenomenon emerging from
failing Helix Gates. See [the_outerlight.md](factions/the_outerlight.md).

**Pellrun-Yth** — Verdantis homeworld and primary Crèche World
restoration site.

**Plenary Fleet** — Consilium navy.

**Quarantine Wardens** — automated Vyrnari guardian ships. See
[quarantine_wardens.md](factions/quarantine_wardens.md).

**Run, the** — a single playthrough of Helixfall. See
[storylines.md](storylines.md).

**Sapien** — baseline humanoids; the galaxy's *default* species. See
[species.md](species.md).

**Sealed Gate** — a Helix Gate that has failed. Three hundred and
counting between the Inner Spiral and the Outer Verge.

**Sealing Crisis** — recurring waves of accelerated Gate failure.

**Seven, the** — informal reference to the *First-Tone Shrines*.

**Slave Wars** — six decades of Mhirsa rebellion against House
Andrachen, ending one hundred and twenty years ago.

**Splice, the** — bio-modder culture. See
[the_splice.md](factions/the_splice.md).

**Spliced** — individual who has taken cross-species grafts. Any
species can become Spliced. See [species.md](species.md).

**Substrate** — the Lattice's distributed computational medium.

**Tainted Heir, the** — Iolen Vellis-Lirath. See above and
[twin_houses_vellis_lirath.md](factions/twin_houses_vellis_lirath.md).

**Throne of Ash** — Iron Concordat flagship dreadnought; default
final boss.

**Throne-line dreadnought** — Iron Concordat heavy warship class.

**Tholai** — Crèche World; Yssari homeworld.

**Tier (of conflict)** — one of the seven canonical conflict layers.
See [conflicts.md](conflicts.md).

**Toll** — fee charged by the Mercatile Verge at every working Gate.

**Twin Houses, the** — Vellis and Lirath, jointly ruling Vassirin.
See [twin_houses_vellis_lirath.md](factions/twin_houses_vellis_lirath.md).

**Unmade, the** — Choral Order heretics who want to *complete* the
Collapse. See [the_unmade.md](factions/the_unmade.md).

**Vassirin** — homeworld of the Twin Houses.

**Vela Abandonment** — Consilium decision twenty-three years ago not
to defend twelve outer colonies. Triggered Concordat secession.

**Vellisian / Lirathi** — the two bloodlines of the Twin Houses. See
[species.md](species.md).

**Verdantis, the** — bio-purity religious-ethical movement. See
[the_verdantis.md](factions/the_verdantis.md).

**Vol-Karran** — Crèche World; Ferran homeworld.

**Vor** — radiation-eating species from Inrith. See
[species.md](species.md).

**Vyrnari** — the long-vanished progenitor civilization. They built
the Helix Gates. They left ten thousand years ago.

**Warden Prime** — a particularly large / dangerous Warden, named
when first encountered; sometimes a final-boss variant.

**Whisperborn** — rare species conceived inside an Echo-rift; brief
glimpses of futures. See [species.md](species.md).

**White Cipher** — Lirath-funded mercenary company.

**Yokebreaker Squadron** — Mhirsa Compact hunter-killer fleet.

**Yorin-Sa** — moon where the Lattice first woke up.

**Yssari** — bioluminescent telepathic amphibians. See
[species.md](species.md).
